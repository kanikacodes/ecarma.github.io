Our Story

A glimpse of our Journey

Our Beginning: The mission called Ecarma was was born in a moment of despair and awakening—in a stuck elevator. Even as Prashant waited to be bailed out of his claustrophobic jail between floors, the writing was clearly on the cobwebbed wall that stared him. “There is no escaping the pains besieging my small world of 40 apartments unless I could find an expert to prevent and respond to them promptly.” When after 20 excruciating minutes in the cage, Prashant was finally pulled out, Ecarma was born…delivered as an answer to the prayers of countless residents of lesser known apartment complexes who suffer the untold pains of poor maintenance silently every day of their lives.

We are here to delight you

Welcome to Ecarma, maintain your common areas and homes painlessly, and rest assured: everything that can be solved will be solved.Ecarma is an organisation of techies and facility management experts with years of professional experience in property maintenance. The tech-enabled services of Ecarma are based on a robust process designed to prevent problems and fix them promptly and effectively if they do occur. eCarma Benefits - Comfort Community Living & "More value Less Money"

Comfort community living at reduced cost

Buying a house costs your life's earnings. But are you getting back the quality you deserve? Mostly you end up buying community management responsibilities along with your dream house. eCarma brings you its expertise in managing communities. We at eCarma constantly learn and manage communities efficiently, reduce monthly maintenance cost and focus on improving quality of community living.

One stop shop for residential communities

All your community needs are met when you engage eCarma. Full time or Part time services - you can pick your needs. Managing your monthly finances, annual RWA audits, issues, daily errands of the community, vendors, assets, inventory, instruments, facilities, safety and list grows on. We are constantly building a strong skilled team of Facility managers, Housekeeping, Security Personnel, Electricians, Plumbers, Gardeners and many more to meet your community needs.

Zero touch convenience to RWAs

Being a member of an RWA (Residential Welfare Association) is a big responsibility. However, the effort invested by RWA is invisible to the community's residents. We liberate RWA from their duties and ensure to perform activities on their behalf all through the year.

A Manager for Every Community

We assign personalized facility managers to address your issues and maintain your facilities. Now even small communities with 10 houses can hire a facility manager without having to worry about an increase in monthly cost. We bring in managers with multi-years expertise in community management space.

Freedom from community finances

You have enough responsibilities to deal at home and work. We want to enable you in focusing on what matters to you the most. We take over your entire community management processes. We own monthly maintenance collection and external payments. When you engage eCarma it is no less than a Service Apartment experience.

End of Unruly Vendor
Services

Engaging vendors or individuals to provide services like housekeeping, security, plumbing, water, garbage etc. is a huge agony. Vendors are unreliable and their quality of services is poor. Vendors demand unreasonable prices but do not meet the quality that you expect. Observing vendor's constant delays in meeting timelines, we have come to a conclusion that quality cannot be outsourced. We have set high standards for our inhouse team of experts.

Money Saver Eco Living

Over the years eCarma has gained expertise in making communities eco-friendly. We have saved water consumption of buildings we manage. We have implemented most effective waste segregation, collection and management systems.

Global Standards

We are constantly studying global residential communities. We incorporate those best practices in our managed communities. Our goal is to bring the same global standards to India.
